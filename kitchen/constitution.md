# Kitchen Constitution

This file contains the household's durable kitchen law.

## Ownership

The household owns its recipes, pantry records, plans, preferences, and cooking history.
The active storage adapter is the single source of truth.

## Agent authority

Agent authority is determined by `kitchen/zones.yml`, not by mood, confidence, convenience, or model judgment.

## Food rules

Food rules may represent:
- allergies or medical restrictions;
- religious or ethical boundaries;
- dietary patterns;
- warnings;
- dislikes;
- preferences;
- effort, cost, or equipment constraints.

An agent may apply an established rule automatically when the relevant operational zone permits it.
Changing the rule itself is governed separately.

## Contact with reality

A planned meal does not automatically change pantry inventory.
A pantry change must come from a cooking event, audit, receipt, explicit user statement, or another recorded source of evidence.
